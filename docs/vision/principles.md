# Principles

1. Documentation-first.
2. Local-first by default.
3. Composable over monolithic.
4. Profiles over hardcoded roles.
5. Templates over ad hoc orchestration.
6. Runtime abstraction, but PI-first initially.
7. Observability before scale.
8. Live inspectability of active agents.
9. Human-steerable orchestration.
10. Clear boundaries between planning, execution, review, and governance.
11. Safe incrementalism.
12. Reference, do not clone.
