# Overstory Notes

## Focus Areas

- hierarchy and delegation model,
- role boundaries,
- documentation and workflow decomposition.

## Key Takeaways

- strong orchestrator-to-lead-to-worker framing helps maintain coordination clarity,
- explicit role separation reduces cross-role drift,
- structured documentation is critical to reproducible agent workflows.

## Reuse in SPORE

Use role hierarchy concepts and discipline, but keep SPORE config-driven and domain-aware.
